Take the following brain dump and structure it:

## Themes
Group the thoughts into logical themes. 3-6 themes max.

## Key Points
Under each theme, bullet-point the key ideas. Keep the original phrasing where possible.

## Priorities
Identify 1-3 things that seem most important or urgent.

## Open Questions
List any gaps or unresolved thoughts that need follow-up.

Don't add interpretation. Don't judge the ideas. Just organise them.
